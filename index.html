SCEM Portal Guide
Everything you need to know
📋 Core Features
Dashboard
Your home screen showing stats, latest announcements, a mini calendar, and upcoming events.
Announcements
Browse all school announcements. Filter by category and sort by date or name.
Calendar
Full monthly calendar view showing all events mapped to their dates.
Search & Filter
Search announcements by keyword and use category filters to narrow results.
Categories
Academic, Event, and Outside Campus announcement types.
Profile
View your account info including name, email, role, and birthday.
🛠️ Admin-Only Features
Create Post
Post announcements with title, description, category, date, time, venue, and person in charge.
Pin Announcements
Pin important announcements so they always appear at the top of the list for all users.
Image Uploads
Attach images to announcements (max 5 MB). Viewable full-screen.
Delete Announcements
Remove announcements permanently from the manage grid.

Admin accounts have full access to create, edit, pin, and delete announcements. Student accounts can view and search all content but cannot post or modify it.
